About


How we began

One fine October morning, with the cool wind blowing outside, a Khan Academy user named TorrentForce had an idea as cool as the outdoor temperature.

This particular user had been on multiple teams, such as CodeX, Scriptara, and Team Coding Pros. He had had so much fun on those teams. But when they fell inactive, TorrentForce got bored.
His idea was: He would make his own team!

So TorrentForce set to work, creating a team homepage using Python, the programming language he knew the most.

Once that was done, TorrentForce invited several users to join the team. All accepted, and Team Omega Primes had officially begun.

The terrible inactivity

Soon after the team's success, however, TorrentForce announced that he would become inactive! Soon after TorrentForce fell inactive, so did the entire team! It looked as if Team Omega Primes was on the verge of falling apart.

The team rises again

Thankfully, under the leadership of MMS Matthew360°, Team Omega Primes continued to stand.

Then, TorrentForce returned, with new knowledge of HTML and CSS! He and Hooray X. built a new homepage using HTML and CSS.
Soon, after the team's victory, Pinkcatsoda, a skilled programmer-artist, also joined the team. It seemed that the glorious days would never end.

The Christmas Blizzard

But around Christmas, the team faced yet another crucial challenge. Then important admin M.M.S. (Matthew360) went semi-inactive, soon followed by Pinkcatsoda and TorrentForce too. Just when things were looking terrible, DebugRanger decided to join because he thought this team needed help. Lord Familia and 1001programmer1001 joined too.
With the help of the new members, the team rose again, with important admin Hooray X. updating the homepage to look even cooler!

The New Year Rush

Since the start of the new year, 3 members have joined Team Omega Primes within the first week of 2026! This was probably one of T.O.P.'s greatest weeks. Say hello to 1001programmer1001, Lord Familia, and Fi fi fo fum!

Contest Summoning

Soon, new users started swarming in thanks to 1001programmer1001's posters and our games. Rain, Lettuce the Great Parrot🥨🦜, Borbonbaseball17, Shadow, Akh,
caleb.catt, Exospacer, JustLord, and Robby joined the team. Hooray X. hosted the Contest: Planetoid and 1001programmer1001 came out on top!

Ocean Exploring

TorrentForce created projects Snake I and Snake II and successfully reached over 30 votes. This victory gave us the idea for a JS project that could rival Team Quantum Cat's CoC final Abyssal. We started the project Ocean Before Time, with most of the team helping. We were surprised how popular it was even when it was a menu without any real game, getting 38 votes. And just like that, we attracted the users Josephine❤︎, GᴀʟᴀxʏRᴀɴɢᴇʀ, ϮԋϵϾԋᴉͼҝϵлѪαϟϯϵπ, and Fluffy (≖⩊≖). At the time, Rain, the Brainstormer Head, created a pixel art poster attracting a portion of the members mentioned above.

HTML Reckoning

While most of the team worked on Ocean before time,Hooray X. and Rain decided to do some HTML.As Hooray X. made yet another update on this cool and spectacular homepage,Rain created inspirational posters to Join T.O.P.. Hooray X. started making a 3D Platformer and together with Rain's posters to get 6 new members,SWΛNKYᴍᴀɴ ,Ϊ∯Λ∀ΞΠΘΠΛ₼Ξ,Anonymous,Ikorac🔥 ,Blueburst ,FancyBoy and Dragon Studios🔥🐦🐉🐍

Tragic Turns

Right after another team success, with progress on Ocean Before Time going well, several rumours appeared, damaging the team. Some outside non-member KA users entered into Team Omega Primes' team chat threads. As a result, discussion turned toward worries about being b@nned, and admins Rain and X. also became b@nned! One member left the team as a result of these sudden events! Team Omega Primes was both at the height of its glory and at the brink of collapse...

Back from the Dead

Rain was b@nned, T.O.P. was fading, and for a moment it looked like the team might finally go quiet. Still, certain members refused to let it end, finding ways to reach each other through GitHub and keep the spirit of Omega Primes alive. Then, all of a sudden, our newest member, Firestar. Rain later told us that he had stepped back from the admin team and passed his place to GreenFroggy64. After countless days and months of rebuilding, T.O.P. was back from the dead.

Coding Levels

				
These coding levels determine your programming skill progress in Team Omega Primes.

				
Alpha Coder

				
Alpha coders have basic skill and may need assistance from more advanced programmers.

				
Pro-alpha coder

				
Pro-alpha coders are improving quickly and can complete small-to-medium tasks more independently.

				
Deltagrammer

				
Deltagrammers are capable and can build many projects with little or no assistance.

				
Pro-deltagrammer

				
Pro-deltagrammers are strong contributors who consistently deliver reliable coding work.

				
Sigma-deltagrammer

				
Sigma-deltagrammers are high-level coders trusted with harder tasks and advanced teamwork.

				
Proto-omicronner

				
Proto-omicronners are near-top coders who are preparing for full omicronner responsibility.

				
Omicronner

				
Omicronners are advanced and highly skilled programmers who help guide less advanced members.

				
Special Rank Ladder

				
After Omicronner, leadership and admin titles continue as: Co-leader/head, Leader/Head, Pro-admin,
					Admin, and Omega Prime.

				

				
Do not be afraid to ask for help, even if you are an Omicronner!
